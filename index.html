🌱 Understanding Environmental Impact

How we calculate the environmental benefits of solar generation
🌳Trees Equivalent

The number of mature trees needed to absorb the same amount of CO₂ avoided through solar generation.

Trees = Energy (kWh) × 0.045
🏠Homes Powered

How many homes could be powered with this clean energy, based on typical consumption of ~9.8 kWh per household daily.

Households = Energy (kWh) × 0.102
⛏️kg Coal Saved

The amount of coal that would need to be burned to generate the same electricity.

Coal Saved = Energy (kWh) × 0.548 kg
💧Litres Water Saved

Water saved by using solar instead of conventional power plants.

Water Saved = Energy (kWh) × 1.4 L
